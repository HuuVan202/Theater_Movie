Refund Policy

Learn about the terms and process for movie ticket refunds at MoonCinema.

Time
Cancel tickets 2 hours before showtime
Cancellation Fee
10,000 VND/ticket
Processing Time
3-7 business days
Eligible for Refund
  • Cancel tickets at least 2 hours before showtime
  • Technical issues from the cinema
  • Payment system errors
Not Eligible for Refund
  • Cancel tickets within 2 hours before showtime
  • Already used tickets, ticket codes, or gift cards
  • Tickets exchanged for promotions/theater exchange
  • After 30 days from purchase/receipt date
Refund Process
1
Submit Request
Contact via hotline, email, or online form with ticket code
2
Verification

3
Processing
Approve request and process refund
4
Completion
Refund sent to account within 3-7 business days
Refund Methods
Online Payment
  • Money refunded to debit card
  • E-wallet/wallet partner after payment
  • Bank transfer to bank account
Counter Payment
  • Cash: Cash refund at counter
  • Bank transfer (if requested)
  • Voucher for next order (if agreed)
Special Cases
  • Canceled Screening: When the cinema cancels a screening due to technical issues or force majeure, customers are entitled to a 100% refund or free exchange to another screening.
  • Promotional Tickets: Tickets purchased with discounts, free vouchers, or gift certificates are typically non-refundable. Only applicable for exchange to other screenings (if available).
  • Lost/Damaged/QR Code Tickets: Customers can verify by providing the transaction code and ID at the customer service counter.
